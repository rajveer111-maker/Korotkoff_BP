This report documents the active physical impact of the ultrasound pulser on **both the RF signal magnitude (amplitude) and the RF signal phase (angle)** when coupled to the human body.

## 3. Conclusion
The ultrasound does not just modulate one property; it drives **dual-mode acousto-RF modulation (AM + PM)**. The phase contains the primary path length changes (useful for tissue displacement/vitals), while the magnitude contains the localized dielectric/impedance shifts. Both show a massive, clear increase in variance (up to $976\text{x}$) when the ultrasound is turned ON.
